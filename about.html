About us

I.O.C Iraq Oil company, a national production company in Baghdad.

Proliferation at the provincial and global levels


we are operating mainly in Baghdad region with the capability and expertise to explore and operate throughout all Iraq and other countries.

Work safe


Our mission is to work safely to increase reserves of oil and gas through drilling and acquisitions, to efficiently produce and optimize recovery of existing reserves to achieve long term profitability.

Preserving the environment


To develop environmental studies resulting from various oil activities and to find appropriate solutions to reduce and control pollution, in addition to carrying out environmental and radiological surveys for all oil activities, and to monitor the implementation of national and international laws, regulations and legislations related to the preservation of the environment and follow up adherence to international environmental conventions and the impact of its decisions on the oil industry.

Why ICO?

Covering a large area of Iraq and the best in the production of crude oil and natural gas free of impurities. We always strive to satisfy our customers and customers in terms of service and product.
